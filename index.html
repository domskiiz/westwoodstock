Welcome to the Second Annual Westwoodstock Student Music Festival! We'll be showcasing the amazing talent of UCLA student musicians in a ten hour music festival, right on Broxton Avenue. The event will take place on Saturday June 2nd from 3:00pm to 1:00am - an all-day block party! We'll also be partnering with local restaurants and bars to offer special drink menus for the 21+ crowd.

Westwoodstock is an annual student music festival produced by House of Balloons, a group of four UCLA students and alumni (Jonathan Liu '18, Tim Connors '18, John Colarusso '18, Sriram Ramikrishnan '17) who share a passion for music and event production. House of Balloons has a proven track record of throwing unique and exciting music events, including the first annual Westwoodstock, Merry Litmas, Trust the Culture, and Rhythm & Booze. Music event production is what we know and we want to bring that passion to the greater Westwood and UCLA community.
